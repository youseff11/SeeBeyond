Our Policies

At ByBeyond, we are committed to providing an excellent shopping experience for our customers. Our policies include information on shipping, returns, privacy, and more. Please read these policies carefully to understand your rights and responsibilities as a customer.

1. Shipping and Delivery Policy

We strive to process and ship all orders as quickly as possible. Shipping times vary based on the delivery destination and the selected shipping method. You will be provided with a tracking number for your order once it has been shipped to monitor its status.

  • Processing Time: 1-2 business days.
  • Estimated Delivery Times: 3-7 business days (domestic), 7-14 business days (international).
  • Shipping Costs: Vary by weight and destination, displayed at checkout.

2. Return and Exchange Policy

Your satisfaction is our priority. If you are not satisfied with your purchase for any reason, you can return the product within 14 days from the date of receipt, provided the product is in its original condition, unused, and with the original invoice.

  • Products must be unused and in their original packaging.
  • Opened or used products cannot be returned, except in case of a manufacturing defect.
  • The customer bears the shipping costs for returns, unless the product is defective.
  • Refunds will be processed within 7 business days after receiving and inspecting the returned product.

3. Privacy Policy

We are committed to protecting the privacy of your personal data. Information is collected solely for the purpose of enhancing your shopping experience and processing your orders. We will not share your personal information with any third party without your explicit consent.

  • Information We Collect: Name, address, email, phone number.
  • How We Use Your Information: To process orders, improve service, and send promotional offers (with your consent).
  • Security: We use encryption technologies to protect your sensitive data.

4. Terms and Conditions of Use

By using our website, you agree to abide by the terms and conditions listed below. These terms govern your use of the site and provide information about your rights and responsibilities.

  • All content on this website is protected by copyright.
  • The website is available for personal, non-commercial use only.
  • We reserve the right to modify these terms at any time.
